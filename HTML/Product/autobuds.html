Auto-Buds

The Auto-Buds were introduced as a revolutionary product, a solution in a natural, evolutionary process. For years, humanity had been making exponential leaps in technology, solving socieal and economic problems and closing the gap between Sci-Fi and reality.

With how inter-connected our world was becoming, and globalisation on the rise, we brought forward a product, an idea, that would push us into the next century.
Communication, the one thing that allowed to progress as we did and our most valuable tool, was being left behind in this age of progrss. We introduced the Auto-Buds, a pair of wireless earbuds that can translate any language as they are spoken.

Auto-Buds: Features

Precision design
The Auto-Buds were designed and built from the ground up wih comfort and ergonomics in mind. With our patented dynamic In-Ear design, the Auto-Buds guarantee a perfect fit, regardless of ear size.
Enhanced Audio Pickup
Our partnership with Audio-technica has allowed us to create a new generation of small form-factor microphones. With increased range for audio detection, patented Active Noise Cancellation and Audio focus, the Auto-Buds are suitable for any situation.
Touch Respose
Fitted with touch sensitive side pads, the Auto-Buds can be controlled with swipe gestures. Switch through your playlists or control output volume with ease.
Assistant Integration
Have your pick between Google's Assistant and Amazon's Alexa as a virtual assistant and seamlessly integrate the Auto-Buds into your workflow.
Context Awareness
Auto-Buds use state of the art machine learning technology to ensure that your translations are of the best possible quality. The translation software utilises keyword matching to ensure that the translation software gives the contextually correct translations.
