Auto-Buds

The Auto-Buds were introduced as a revolutionary product, a solution in a natural, evolutionary process. For years, humanity had been making exponential leaps in technology, solving socieal and economic problems and closing the gap between Sci-Fi and reality.
With how inter-connected our world was becoming, and globalisation on the rise, we brought forward a product, an idea, that would push us into the next century.
Communication, the one thing that allowed to progress as we did and our most valuable tool, was being left behind in this age of progrss. We introduced the Auto-Buds, a pair of wireless earbuds that can translate any language as they are spoken.
