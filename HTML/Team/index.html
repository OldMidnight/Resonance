The Team

Members

Our founding team consists of Dublin City University students Aifric Nolan, Fareed Idris, Conor Lacey, Sean Dyer and Conor McGovern. We study a mixture of Computer Applications and Enterprise Computing.

We met each other after being placed in a group together for the CA115 module. Our common interests led us to pursue the product Auto-Buds, a state of the art translation device.

Inspiration

We got inspiration from a series of talks that we received over the course of CA115 about machine translation and machine learning.

Auto-pods make heavy use of the cutting-edge machine translation technology that we were introduced to by guest speakers during the talks. Of note is the talk that we received about the Brasiliator tool, which was organised by the ADAPT centre.

Auto-pods also make heavy use of the machine learning technology that we were introduced to. This technology is used to ensure that users are getting translations that are relevant to their ongoing conversation.

The primary market for this product is business people, particularly those that travel a large amount for work. We decided to pick this market as our primary target as a result of a talk that we received on e-commerce.
