Our Story

Resonance was founded by Aifric Nolan, Fareed Idris, Conor Lacey, Sean Dyer and Conor McGovern. Their story began when they started studying in Dublin City University in Ireland. They shared a module together called Digital Innovation and Management Enterprise, in which they were instructed to design a product together. This is when the company was formally founded. Inspired by the design of their first product, Auto-Translate, they founded Resonance on the 1st of March 2018. Working from the college computer laboratories, they built a product that would set them on a path to success.

Fareed orignially came up with the company name Resonance, and the others instantly fell in love with it. It was decided. This was the name that would be well-known for generations to come. Initially, their main marketing strategies were mere posters.

After some time, Resonance expanded rapidly and caught the attention of not only the academic community, but many investors as well. The company has grown beyond the dreams of any of the founders, and is the stepping stone to a new era of technological innovation. Resonance invents at the boundary of technology to make amazing experiences possible, with a dedication to success.

Our Culture

Resonance is widely known for its open and unique culture. This environment awards creative problem solving and rapid decision-making. The open culture keeps people informed. Developers at Resonance work in small teams and move fast to develop new products, constantly iterating and improving. Our core values are shared by all of our developers, striving for a common aim in delivering the best experience possible to our customers. We firmly believe that a good culture promotes happy employees which ultimaltley leads to happy customers. Our employees are known to be driven, talented and among the best of the best. With a focus on teamwork and open communication, we create a competitive atmosphere that fosters personal growth and learning. We go out of our way to give our employees challenging projects and provide the trust and support to meet those challenges successfully.

Statistics

  • As of 2018, Resonance has 5.4 million active customers
  • The company now has 12,000 employees worldwide
  • Forbes Magazine ranked Resonance as third as one of the Best Companies To Work For
  • Our main product; Auto-Translate, now translates over 60 languages
