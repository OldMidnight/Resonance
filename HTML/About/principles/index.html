Our Leadership Principles

These principles are utilised everyday throughout our company and our an essential component of the Resonance culture.

  1. Customer:
     Our policy is to start with the customer and work backward, providing the best possible experience for you.


  2. Invent:
     We require innovation and invention from our teams. We are externally aware and look for new ideas from anywhere.


  3. Learn:
         We are active learners and are always seeking to improve ourselves. Constantly curious about new possibilites.


  4. Develop The Best:
         We recognise exceptional talent and raise the performance bar with every hire and promotion. We develop Leaders.


  5. High Standards:
     We have very high standards and are continually raising the bar and driving our teams to deliver a high quality service.


  6. Ambition:
     We encourage people to be bold and to aspire beyond their dreams. We encourage our teams to think differently.


  7. Trust:
     We treat our customers respectfully and listen attentively. We are very self-critical and will strive for feedback.


  8. Strength:
     We challenge decisions and will question assumptions. We always work with conviction.


  9. Deliver Results:
     We focus on the key inputs of our business and deliver them with the right quality and in a timely fashion. We always rise to the occasion and never settle.
