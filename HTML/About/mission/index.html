Our Mission

Allowing you to connect with the world

Diversity And Inclusion

At Resonance, we believe in oppurtunity for everyone. Our goal is to improve the lives of as many people as possible. We know that diverse perspectives strengthen us as a society, from our products allowing global communication to our support of organisations fighting systematic discrimination, we are commited to building a more diverse and inclusive society.
We are a company of people who bring varying backgrounds, ideas and points of view to inventing on behalf of our cutomers. Our diverse ideaologies come from many sources including race, gender, age, national origin, sexual orientation, culture, education, as well as professional and life experience. We are working to develop leaders and to shape future talent pools to help us meet the needs of our customers around the world.

Job Creation And Investment

Resonance is a powerful engine of growth for the national economy, revitalising communities across Ireland through jo creation, economic investment, and innovative services. Resonance now has 20,000 employees in Dublin. We take in thousands of interns each year from around the globe and provide them with an educational experience unmatched anywhere else.
The way we work is changing. As new technologies continue to unfold in the workplace, over a third of jobs are likely to require skills that are today uncommon in the workplace. Demographic changes and shifts in labor participation will mean future generations will find new ways to sustain economic growth. These changes create oppurtunities to think about how work can continue not just to be a source of income, but purpose and meaning for individuals and communities. Technology can help us seize these oppurtunites. Our goal is to help people connect more with eachother.
